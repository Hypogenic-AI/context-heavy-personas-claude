# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Deep Read)

1. **Persona Vectors: Monitoring and Controlling Character Traits in Language Models** (2507.21509)
   - File: `2507.21509_persona_vectors.pdf`
   - Authors: Chen, Arditi, Sleight, Evans, Lindsey (Anthropic)
   - Year: 2025
   - Why relevant: Provides the mechanistic framework (linear persona directions) and tools for measuring persona activation in latent space. Many-shot prompting results directly relevant.

2. **The Geometry of Persona: Disentangling Personality from Reasoning in LLMs** (2512.07092)
   - File: `2512.07092_geometry_of_persona.pdf`
   - Authors: Wang
   - Year: 2025
   - Why relevant: Shows personality traits as orthogonal linear subspaces. Implies finite-dimensional persona space.

3. **PICLe: Eliciting Diverse Behaviors from LLMs with Persona In-Context Learning** (2405.02501)
   - File: `2405.02501_PICLe_persona_icl.pdf`
   - Authors: Choi, Li (ICML 2024)
   - Why relevant: Formalizes persona elicitation task with Bayesian framework. Baseline for few-shot persona elicitation (K=3).

4. **In-Context Learning with Long-Context Models: An In-Depth Exploration** (2405.00200)
   - File: `2405.00200_icl_long_context.pdf`
   - Authors: Bertsch et al. (CMU)
   - Why relevant: Shows ICL performance keeps improving with 2000+ demonstrations. Key evidence that long context unlocks capabilities.
